**What the background doc claims it says:** ALEKS ("Assessment and Learning in Knowledge Spaces") is Knowledge Space Theory deployed in practice — the real-world instantiation of the surmise-relation / outer-fringe machinery.

**What it actually says:** The 2006 chapter connects Doignon–Falmagne KST (knowledge states, surmise relations, the outer fringe / learnable frontier) to its operational form in ALEKS. The 2021 paper, by ALEKS research scientists, documents the system at scale and the engineering gap between the clean theory and deployment.
