AI-drafted KUD outcomes

Know

The CMYK color model and how ink-on-substrate interactions determine final color appearance
Halftone mechanics: screen frequency, dot gain, tone value increase across substrates
Primary print processes and their substrate compatibility constraints

Understand

Why ink formulation choices (tack, viscosity, tinting strength) propagate through reproduction quality and cost
How substrate surface energy affects ink adhesion, dot gain, and trapping

Do

Select and justify a Pantone reproduction standard, establish targets across substrates, and document findings in professional lab report format
Conduct controlled ink and substrate testing, interpret results against industry tolerance, and communicate findings with precision
Threshold concept

“Color is not a property of the file — it is a property of the ink-substrate interaction. Every reproduction decision is a prediction about a physical process.”
