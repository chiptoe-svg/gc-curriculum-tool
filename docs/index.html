The KUD+ Framework — Background and Justification

The academic companion to the Vision: what KUD+ measures, the theoretical traditions it draws on (Backward Design, Constructive Alignment, Bloom's, Competency-Based Education), the depth-scale extension and its evidence rule, foundational competency treatment, and course- and career-path implementation.

Proposal for empirical validation of the framework's output against external occupational data using a dataset of 268 Clemson GC graduates across 43 occupational categories.

Problem-Solving as a Program-Level Emergent Property

Deep treatment of the research on how problem-solving competence actually develops in undergraduate education — domain-specificity, productive failure (with Kapur's 2×2 framework and the domain-general-skills caveat), reflection, transfer — and how the KUD+ framework operationalizes that view into per-course audit probes, a program coverage lens, and a planned scaffolding analysis.
